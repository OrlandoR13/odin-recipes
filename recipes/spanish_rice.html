Spanish Rice

Instant Pot Spanish Rice

Spanish rice, also known as Mexican rice, can be a bit difficult to get right. With this instant pot recipe, it comes out perfect every time with minimal effort.

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/4 cup small onion diced
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp garlic salt
  • 1/4 tsp oregano
  • 2 cups long-grain rice
  • 2/3 cup tomato sauce

Steps

  1. Turn instant pot to saute
  2. Add in olive oil and onions. Saute for 2-3 minutes
  3. Prepare and rinse rise in strainer
  4. Add in chicken stock once onions are soft. Turn off instant pot
  5. Add in seasonings and stir
  6. Add in rice and top with tomato sauce. Do not stir
  7. Place pressure lid on instant pot
  8. Cook on high pressure for 3 minutes. Allow pressure to release naturally
  9. Remove lid after 20-25 minutes and stir rice
