SlowCooker Pork

Easy and delicious slow cooker pork. Just make sure you start it around 7 hours before mealtime. Sourced from yourhomebasedmom.com

Ingredients

  • 2 large sweet onions (sliced)
  • 5 lbs pork shoulder roast
  • 2 tbsp garlic & herb seasoning mix
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 cups chicken broth

Steps

  1. Grease a 6qt slow cooker with cooking oil
  2. Place onions in bottom of slow cooker
  3. Rub pork with seasonings
  4. Place pork on top of onions
  5. Pour broth over roast
  6. Cover and cook on low for 9-10 hours or on high for 6-7 hours. Meat should pull apart easily with a fork
